Our Story

Why I built this.

I started medical school at 29. Left three years later because I couldn't reconcile what I was learning with how patients were actually being treated. The system wasn't designed to find root causes. It was designed to manage symptoms.

I spent the next two decades building healthcare technology companies -- five in total. Bond Technologies. CareSync. Fern Health. Altoida. io-Urology. Each one taught me something about what breaks when you try to scale clinical care with software.

Then in 2018, our house caught fire. My wife needed lung transplants. Conventional medicine couldn't explain what was happening. Functional medicine did. The practitioners who looked at root causes found answers that no one else could.

That experience changed everything. I went back and finished my MD. Got my MBA at UCLA Anderson and NUS Business School. And started building the platform I wished had existed for the providers who saved my wife's life.

BioInsights exists to democratize root-cause medicine. To give every provider the tools, protocols, and AI-powered workflows to deliver the kind of care that saved my family -- without needing 20 years of specialized training to do it.
